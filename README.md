# Atlas OS

Atlas is an AI-powered resale intelligence system designed to identify profitable products, monitor retail opportunities, and help users make faster buying decisions before products sell out.

## Current Focus

Atlas is currently focused on Pokémon resale intelligence, including:

- Pokémon Center product monitoring
- Pokémon TCG market tracking
- Product alerts
- Release calendars
- Opportunity scoring
- Profit analysis
- Daily market briefs

Future expansion may include:

- Disney
- Starbucks
- Lorcana
- One Piece
- Fast-food promotions
- Theme parks
- Seasonal collectibles

## How Atlas Works

```text
Scouts collect product and market data
                ↓
Signals detect meaningful changes
                ↓
Market and pattern systems analyze demand
                ↓
Decision systems score opportunities
                ↓
Atlas creates alerts, reports, and recommendations
                ↓
Runtime data is stored in .atlas_data
